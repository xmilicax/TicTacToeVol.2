Tic Tac Toe Vol.2

This is a advanced version of the popular game Tic Tac Toe.

Rules: Players take turns placing their marks in empty squares on the grid. After each player has placed three marks, their oldest mark is automatically removed when they place a new one. The objective is to align three of your marks in a row — vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. The first player to achieve this wins the game!
May the best player win!
